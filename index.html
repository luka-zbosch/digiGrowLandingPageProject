Mobile app for farmers

DigiGrow is an app designed to help farmers and agricultural organizations make informed decisions that lead to increased crop yield and overall success in agriculture.

The app provides real-time data, comprehensive information, and user-friendly tools to help farmers make the most of their resources and improve their bottom line.

Management accounts also provide important support for farmers in the form of subsidies and grants, helping to ensure that they have the resources they need to succeed.

Main features of DigiGrow include:

Farmers can draw their farms as polygons on a map and view different layers, such as NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index ) and LAI (Leaf Area Index), to monitor crop growth and health. They can also add notes as customized markers on the map to keep track of important information about their crops, such as disease outbreaks or pests' presence.

Agricultural meteograms with detailed weather information, including data like cloud coverage, temperature, spray window, precipitation, and more, are available for up to seven days in advance. This information helps farmers plan and make informed decisions, resulting in improved crop yields and overall success in agriculture.

Reliable and accurate data from trusted providers ensure that farmers have access to the most up-to-date and actionable information available.

Google Maps integration provides a user-friendly interface for farmers to view satellite imagery and other relevant data.
